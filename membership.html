MEMBERSHIP

The membership fee (for bonafide researchers from India) is Rs. 600 per year + Rs 50 for one time registration. The journal is sent free to members in good standing. For others the annual subscription is Rs. 3000 within India. Overseas Subscription charges are US $ 200 + postage (for print) and US$ 250 + postage (for online+print). Subscription for ICAR Institutes, SAU Libraries and Non-profit organizations of India is Rs. 2000 per year. An Indian citizen working in India can also become a life member by paying Rs 7050/- (for details contact General Secretary, NSI).These rates are subject to periodical revision as and when decided by the Society. The society does not accept responsibility for loss during postal transit. Communications regarding the subscriptions for the Indian journal of Nematology and membership of the Nematological society of India should be addressed to the General Secretary, the Nematological Society of India, Division of Nematology, I.A.R.I., New Delhi-110012, India.
